Ground Beef Pizza

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup of sliced onions

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Cook the ground beef in a pan until browned and drain excess fat.
  5. Sprinkle the cooked ground beef over the pizza sauce.
  6. Add sliced bell peppers and onions on top of the beef.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
