Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • 300g lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 200g ricotta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat the ground beef in a pan until browned.
  4. Mix the ricotta cheese with the ground beef.
  5. Layer the lasagna sheets, meat mixture, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
