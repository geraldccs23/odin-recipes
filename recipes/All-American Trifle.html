All- American Trifle

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 box of vanilla cake mix
  • 2 cups of vanilla pudding
  • 1 cup of whipped cream
  • 1 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vanilla cake according to the package instructions and let it cool.
  2. Cut the cooled cake into small cubes.
  3. In a trifle bowl, layer the cake cubes, vanilla pudding, whipped cream, and mixed berries.
  4. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of whipped cream and berries on top.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
